Associations

American Library Association

The ALA is a national organization whose mission is to promote the development and improvement of library and information services, as well as the profession of librarianship, to ensure access to information for all.

Partnership for Academic Library Collaboration & Innovation

PALCI is an organization in Pennsylvania that seeks to connect academic libraries throughout the state and surrounding areas, providing cost-effective and sustainable access to information resources and services.

Associated College Libraries of Central Pennsylvania

The ACLCP connects academic libraries in Central Pennsylvania, providing for easier resource-sharing and access to information between the libraries who are members.

Society of American Archivists

The SAA is a national organization that sets a standard of values and ethics for archivists and those in archival professions, with a commitment to expanding access and developing standards.
