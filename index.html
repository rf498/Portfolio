About Raleigh

Raleigh Frantz (they/them) is a library technician and creative with several years experience in academic libraries. They received their Bachelor’s of Art in Art & Visual Culture from Lebanon Valley College, and are currently pursuing a Master’s of Library and Information Science at Drexel University. Their professional interests include academic libraries, information preservation, special media archives. They live in the-middle-of-nowhere, Pennsylvania. When they’re not in the library or chugging away at schoolwork, you can usually find them writing original fiction, watching horror movies, or playing with their cat, Cobweb.
